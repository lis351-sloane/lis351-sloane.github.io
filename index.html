Sloane Rosen is a Sophomore at The University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Journalism and Communications with a certificate in Sports Communications. Sloane grew up in Weston, Massachusetts. Here at Wisconsin, she is D1 student athlete on the Wisconsin Dance Team. She performs at the Badger football and basketball games. The UW Dance Team also competes at Nationals at ESPN in Orlando every January.

When she is not dancing, she loves to spend time with family and friends. Additionally, she loves to be on camera. In summer 2023, she filmed two commercials. One was in partnership with Under Armour and Her Campus for a movement campaign called ‘Why I Move.’ She also filmed a commercial for the local car dealership, Zimbrick Honda, in Madison.
